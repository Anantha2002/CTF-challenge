Gummy Bear Soup

This delicious soup is made with gummy bears, sugar, and a secret blend of spices! It's sweet and savory, and an absolute hit with kids. Serve it up and watch the smiles appear!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gummy bears
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of your favorite spices
  • 6 cups water

Instructions:

  1. Combine the gummy bears, sugar, and spices in a large pot.
  2. Pour in the water and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Serve and enjoy!

Rainbow Fries

These unique fries are sure to brighten up any meal. They're made with a rainbow of vegetables and spices, and they pack a flavorful punch. Serve them with your favorite dipping sauces!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups potatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup carrots, diced
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1/4 cup zucchini, diced
  • 2 tablespoons of your favorite spices
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. Combine the potatoes, carrots, bell peppers, and zucchini in a large bowl.
  3. Add the spices and olive oil, and mix until everything is evenly coated.
  4. Spread the fries on a baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
